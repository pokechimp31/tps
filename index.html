The Activity

What is ZORBing?

ZORB Soccer!

ZORBing is any physical activity performed inside one of these: a gigantic inflatable ball that protects its wearer while still allowing for mobility. ZORB Soccer, ZORB Sumo Wrestling, and even more ZORB activities are all energetic and are sure to result in a good time.

  • The ZORB Itself A giant inflatable orb — roughly 1.5 meters in diameter — with an inner pod cushioned by an air layer that absorbs impacts and rolls.
  • The Experience Do whatever suits your interests! A wide range of activities can also be done (better) in a ZORB!
  • Great for Groups ZORBing is naturally social — spectators cheer, riders compete, everyone gets involved. Perfect for school events, fundraisers, and club meetups.
  • Safe & Accessible No special skills or fitness required. The inflatable cushion keeps riders safe, making it one of the most inclusive physical activities. (Anyone on the football team will probably do pretty well, though.)
  • A Fundraising Goldmine At just $200 per ZORB, each one can generate multiples of its cost at a single event. We're building a fleet every club at Pennington can use.
Who We Are

TPS ZORB

ZORB Club is a club centered around the collective ownership of ZORBs. Partnering with other clubs and events and meeting on the weekends, we strive for connection. Everyone is welcome. No matter your interests, participation, or ability to ZORB, you can help. Together, we are ZORB Club.
