Who We Are

TPS ZORB

ZORB Club is a club centered around the collective ownership of ZORBs. Partnering with other clubs and events and meeting on the weekends, we strive for connection. Everyone is welcome. No matter your interests, participation, or ability to ZORB, you can help. Together, we are ZORB Club.

Join Today Join ZORB Club and automatically receive the title of Co-Founder — a credential you can put on your resume or transcript. We are building something from the ground up, and your name will be part of it from day one.
